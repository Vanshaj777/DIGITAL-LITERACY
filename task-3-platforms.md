Activity Proof Notes
To fulfill the collaboration requirement, I developed a Digital Literacy and Online Safety Quiz using Google Forms. I collected a total of 5 responses from my peers to gauge their understanding of cybersecurity fundamentals.

Data Collection (Google Forms): As shown in the pie chart, I tested users on password security. Interestingly, the data revealed that 60% of respondents incorrectly believe using the same strong password for all accounts is a secure practice, while only 20% correctly identified the use of a unique passphrase and a password manager as the most secure method.

Data Organization (Google Sheets): The responses were automatically synced to a linked Google Sheet. This provided a structured view of timestamps, multiple-choice answers, and short-answer definitions for terms like "Digital Footprint."

Academic & Technical Value
This activity highlights the power of cloud collaboration. As a CSE AIML student, the ability to collect and clean data via Google Workspace is a precursor to data analysis. These tools will help me academically by allowing me to conduct surveys for research projects and manage group assignments efficiently. It also reinforced the need for digital awareness, as the survey results clearly show that even tech-savvy students can have misconceptions about online safety.
