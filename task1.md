This infographic, titled "Digital Literacy," is designed as a clean and professional educational guide for students, using a creative "notebook and stationary" aesthetic.

Key Sections of the Image:
01: Definition: It defines Digital Literacy as the essential ability to find, evaluate, and share information across various digital platforms.

02: Useful Digital Tools: It highlights industry-standard tools for students, including Notion/Trello for organization, Google Scholar/ResearchGate for academic research, and GitHub/Kaggle for technical and AI development.

03: Safe Internet Practices: It provides actionable safety advice, specifically recommending Two-Factor Authentication (2FA), strong passwords, and a crucial warning about UPI safety (never entering a PIN to receive money).
